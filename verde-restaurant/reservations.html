What to know before you book

Table Hold Time

We hold your table for 15 minutes from your booking time. If you're running late, please call us so we can accommodate you.

Cancellations

Please cancel or modify at least 24 hours in advance. Same-day cancellations should be made by phone.

Large Groups

For 10+ guests, please call directly or enquire about our private dining options. We'll take good care of you.

Please note any dietary requirements in your booking. We'll inform the kitchen before your arrival to ensure a great experience.
