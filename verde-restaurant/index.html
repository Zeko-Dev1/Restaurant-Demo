Our Story

Where honest cooking
meets a considered table

Founded in 2015 by Chef Marko Ilievski, Verde was born from a simple belief: that the best meal is one that respects its ingredients, its seasons, and its guests.

We don't follow trends. We follow what grows. Our menu changes with the markets, driven by relationships with local farms and producers who share our values. Every dish on the table started as a conversation — with a farmer, a fisherman, a winemaker.

  • Seasonal menus — updated weekly, not quarterly
  • Relationships with producers within 150km where possible
  • 60 covers — intimate enough to cook with care
  • Wine list built around small-production natural growers

The Kitchen

Cooking from
the ground up

Chef Marko Ilievski trained across kitchens in Lyon, Copenhagen, and Barcelona before returning home to open Verde in 2015. His cooking is deeply rooted in the produce itself — minimal intervention, maximum clarity of flavour.

Working closely with Sous Chef Elena Petrov, the kitchen at Verde operates with a rare combination of precision and warmth. Dishes are designed to be genuinely delicious — never clever for its own sake.

"

The best thing I can do with a perfect piece of fish is get out of its way. Seasoning, heat, and a plate that lets it speak. That's cooking.

— Chef Marko Ilievski
