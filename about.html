Our Story

Cooking with conviction

Restaurant was founded on a single belief: that a great meal starts long before it reaches the table — with the relationships, the seasons, and the hands that grow the food.

The Beginning

From a single conviction
to sixty seats

In 2015, Chef Marko Ilievski returned home after a decade cooking across Europe with one ambition: to open a restaurant where the ingredient — not the technique — was the star.

He found a space in the city's old quarter, hired four people he trusted, and opened Restaurant on a Tuesday in October with a handwritten menu and forty covers. The city noticed immediately.

Nine years later, Restaurant has grown to sixty seats and a team of fourteen, but the philosophy hasn't changed. Every menu is written by what's available that week. Every producer relationship has been built over years, not spreadsheets. The room still feels like it belongs to its guests the moment they sit down.

What We Stand For

The pillars we cook by

Seasonality

Our menu changes with what grows, not what sells. We rewrite the menu every week around what our producers have available — no item is permanent.

Relationships

Every supplier on our list is a relationship, not a vendor. We visit our farms. We know our fishermen. We taste the olive oil before we serve it.

Transparency

We tell you where the lamb is from, who grew the tomatoes, and where the produce came from. We believe knowing the story makes it taste better.

Genuine Hospitality

We don't do scripted service. Our team knows the menu deeply, notices the table, and responds to what you actually need — not what the checklist says.

Sourcing & Sustainability

Where our food
comes from

The majority of what we serve at Restaurant travels less than 150 kilometres to reach your plate. That's not a marketing claim — it's a kitchen policy.

We work with a small, carefully selected group of producers who share our values. Seasonal organic vegetables from a family farm to the east. Sustainable day-boat fish from the coast. Heritage breed meat from a third-generation farm thirty kilometres away.

We're not perfect, and we don't claim to be. Some things — good olive oil, exceptional vinegar, specific grains — come from further away because quality demands it. But everything on the menu has a story we're happy to tell you.

Timeline

Nine years in the making

2015
Restaurant opens its doors

Chef Marko Ilievski opens Restaurant in October with a 40-cover dining room and a handwritten menu. Fully booked within two weeks of launch.

2017
Elena Petrov joins as Sous Chef

The kitchen evolves significantly with Elena's arrival — a new dessert programme launches and the menu becomes more structured and ambitious.

2018
The Garden Room opens

Restaurant expands into the adjacent space, creating the private Garden Room — available for private dining for up to 18 guests.

2020
Through the storm

The pandemic tests the team's resilience. Restaurant pivots to a produce box and meal kit programme that keeps producer relationships alive and the team together.

2021
Chef's Table launches

The intimate 8-guest Chef's Table experience launches to immediate acclaim — offering guests a ringside seat to watch the kitchen at work while dining.

2024
620 reviews. Still cooking.

Restaurant reaches 620 verified guest reviews with a 4.8 average. The menu still changes weekly. Nothing has been taken for granted. The next chapter is just beginning.
