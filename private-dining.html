The Garden Room

A room designed for celebration

The Garden Room is Restaurant's dedicated private dining space — a warm, softly lit room set apart from the main dining floor. It seats up to 18 guests around a single table or configured as a standing reception for up to 25.

Full AV capability for presentations, custom menus, dedicated service team, and a private entrance for discreet arrivals. Available Tuesday through Saturday evenings.

What's Included
  • Dedicated server and sommelier
  • Custom printed menu cards
  • Welcome drinks and canapés on arrival
  • Bespoke menu created with Chef Marko
  • Full AV setup for presentations
  • Floral arrangement (optional, quoted separately)
Minimum Spend
From $1,200 (weekday) · $1,600 (Friday–Saturday)

The Chef's Table

Ringside to the kitchen

The Chef's Table seats up to 8 guests at a table positioned in direct view of Restaurant's kitchen pass. This is the most intimate experience we offer — guests dine as the kitchen works around them, with dishes presented directly by the team.

Chef Marko designs an exclusive multi-course menu for Chef's Table evenings. No printed menu — courses are presented and explained by the kitchen team as they arrive. Wine pairing is available and strongly recommended.

What's Included
  • Exclusive 7-course chef's menu
  • Direct service from kitchen team
  • Wine pairing available (quoted separately)
  • Ingredient tours and kitchen conversation
  • Welcome cocktail on arrival
Minimum Spend Per Person
From $130 per person (food only)
Wine pairing: $65–$95 per person additional
Enquire: Chef's Table
